Biscuit: a piece of random passwords

Biscuit is a big, printable table with 260 passwords generated from a single ID string.

Biscuit helps you to pick a random password for important purposes, e.g. smartcard PINs. With less than 0.4% probability in one guess, even after 3 attempts it's unlikely to crack!
